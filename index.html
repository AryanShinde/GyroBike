A self balancing bike, basically some attractive facts or information about the bike.

"GyroBike reinvents the training wheel!"

It aims to replace traditional training wheels or stabilizers and to eradicate the bad habits that they teach kids when learning to ride a bike - leaning away from a turn and constantly putting a foot down at the first sign of a wobble when they're eventually taken off.
A Jyrobike (formerly known as Gyrobike)vh also is a bicycles grsrrr with a special front wheel designed to make balancing easier. ... The special front wheel contains a rotating flywheel driven by a rechargeable-battery-powered motor that spins at high RPMs like a gyroscope.
